Qualification

Education and Training: Our extraordinary range of Education and Training qualifications present any learner with the ability to gain skills and knowledge from any area of providing lesson from looking-out to Mentoring to Equality and Diversity. This sector is becoming increasingly wider and we have made sure that we are furnished with all required tools and awards available to ensure any learner is successful in their chosen qualification and on their chosen career path. Within this education industry sector include: Primary and Secondary schools, Advance Education and Higher Education posts, University teaching, adult education institutes (i.e. evening classes), hospitals, prisons and detention Centre, professional bodies and training organizations. Health and Social Care: APRB’s qualifications are competent and designed to meet the needs and capabilities of those who are working within any Social Care sector, or for those who are looking to set up their career. Our comprehensive portfolio of courses include a variety of courses from the safely handling of Medicines to Awareness of both Mental and Physical Health Problems. In case of Health and Social care sector, when we talk about it, more often you are not going into this having no attractive packages offered but the satisfying nature of the work and positive effects and outcomes that you can have in the job. If anybody want a career with promotion, progression, having job security, and get an enormous sense of individual achievement from knowing you are helping others – then certainly, this sector is waiting for him. Working with the health care and social care sector has achieved numerous opportunities at all levels, from not only full and part-time positions but also from assistants to management level. Some examples of potential career paths include:

  • Care worker – It is designed to assist people who need support with daily needs to live independently.

  • Social worker – It is designed to provide the support and guidance for people who need care and favor, including those with mental and/or physical sicknesses, disabilities and various problems related with age.

  • Community support worker - It is designed with giving support with indirect needs for example, teaching and training people to cook for themselves, arranging and planning their tours and usually helping them with day to day living.

First Aid: It is recommended for the workplace by replacing the approval system of First Aid Qualification that it should be approved by Ofqual regulated Awarding Organizations.First Aid Qualifications have been produced in counsel with the HSE and the qualification regulators and are approved by Skills for Good Health. APRB are also approved by the Health and Safety Executive (HSE) to make available for the new EFAW and FAW qualifications for the purpose of Health and Safety (First Aid) law.

Hospitality and Catering:If anybody want to start a career within the Hospitality and Catering industry then our portfolio of qualifications have been fixed and designed with industry experts to assist any learner to develop their skills and knowledge to be successful in their careers. APRB decorates the learner’s study curriculum not only from cookery to food safety but also the catering principles to food and beverage services.

APRB has a wide range of qualifications to suit everyone who is looking forward to pursue a new career with hospitality and catering. Candidates who has completed any of our Hospitality and Catering qualifications with Chef, Baker, Restaurant Manager etc. has plenty of career opportunities with lot of biggest money earning industry sector in the world. Baby care: There are lot of sectors of qualifications which are essential for leading a better life for the people. But the training and practice are much more important to get the best result from this sector. Child or baby care is one of them and it’s very important because they are the future of a nation. To be successful within this childcare sector our qualitative awards and qualifications are ready to make you perfect providing the suitable knowledge, skills, principles and methods. APRB’s qualifications ensure your staff has the skills they need because we believe that occupational competency is an important issue. Computing and It: Computing and It: Statistics show that 21 million people use IT every day at work. Now a days, IT is one of the most rapid changing sectors all round the world and the time has come to keep the eyes on this sector. APRB's fantastic Computing and IT qualifications provide learners with the effective and suitable tools to develop their skills and realizing of ICT. Our extra ordinary Computing and IT qualifications helps our learners to learn more about digital technology and how it can support their job and others as well. Whereas Computing and IT is a big part of so many industry sectors there is a great amount of career opportunities available including Computer and IT Development, Telecoms, Internal Operations, Sales and Marketing, Internet, Installation and Maintenance, Design, IT Project etc. Hair and Beauty: APRB’s hair and beauty sector is designed with three key areas; hairdressing, beauty and make-up. This sector is well established within an industry containing the self-employed, those owning saloons, overseas workers and those who work in the travels and tour industry. There are huge career opportunities that can be established by the help of our range of Hair and Beauty qualifications which include: Stylists, Barbers, Mobile Hairdressers, Saloon Owners, Nail Technician, Holistic Therapist, Beauty Consultant and Make-up Artists.
